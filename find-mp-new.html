Tips for Contacting Your MP

  • Be clear and concise in your communication
  • Include your full name and address
  • Focus on one issue per message
  • Provide relevant documentation if applicable
  • Be polite and professional

Important Notes

  • MPs can only help their own constituents
  • Some issues may need local council involvement
  • Response times can vary based on workload
  • Complex issues may require multiple communications
