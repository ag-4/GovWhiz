Know Your Housing Rights

Tenant Rights

  • Right to live in a property that's safe and in good repair
  • Protection from unfair eviction and unfair rent
  • Right to have your deposit protected
  • Right to challenge excessively high charges
  • Right to know who your landlord is

Council Housing

  • Right to apply for council housing
  • Right to emergency housing if homeless
  • Right to challenge council decisions
  • Right to repair and maintenance
  • Right to buy options

Housing Benefits

  • Universal Credit housing payment
  • Local Housing Allowance
  • Council Tax Reduction
  • Discretionary Housing Payments
  • Support for mortgage interest

Emergency Housing Support

If you're at risk of homelessness or need urgent housing support:

  • Call Shelter's helpline: 0808 800 4444
  • Contact your local council's housing department
  • Visit the nearest Citizens Advice Bureau
