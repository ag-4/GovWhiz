üó≥Ô∏è How to Vote

Your guide to participating in UK democracy

üìù Voter Registration

  • Must be 18+ on polling day
  • British, Irish, or qualifying Commonwealth citizen
  • Register online at gov.uk/register-to-vote
  • Deadline: 12 working days before election

üÜî What You Need

  • Photo ID (from May 2023)
  • Passport, driving licence, or free voter ID
  • Polling card (helpful but not required)
  • Know your polling station location

üìÆ Postal/Proxy Voting

  • Apply for postal vote if you can't attend
  • Proxy voting for specific circumstances
  • Applications must be received by deadline
  • Emergency proxy available in some cases
